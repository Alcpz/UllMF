Detailed Description

Effective distribution of work among different processors: A Workload for 4 processes can be represented as: counts : [1000, 2000, 3000, 4000] displs : [0, 1000, 3000, 6000] num_procs : 4 proportional_workload : [0.1, 0.2, 0.3, 0.4] size: 10000 blocksize : 2.
